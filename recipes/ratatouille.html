Ratatouille

Description

This vegetarian Provencal classic takes tomatoes, eggplant, and squash and turn them into a fresh, herbacous, and summery dish that's so much more than the sum of its parts.

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • 2 eggplants
  • 6 roma tomatoes
  • 2 yellow squashes
  • 2 zucchinis

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil

Herb Seasoning

  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
